Wright Adoption

April 29, 2022

Activated

We are excited to let everyone know that we are officially an “active family” with our consulting agency!

Basically, what this means is that our information can now be presented to birth moms to give them the opportunity to choose us to parent their child! We feel so blessed to be able to move forward with this step and look forward to matching with the birth mother of the child we will eventually bring home!

Thank you so much for your prayers and support up to this point! At this time we are asking for prayers especially for all pregnant moms who are considering adoption as the parenting option for their child! Please pray for them to have wisdom and peace as they make this impossible decision.

Again, thank you for following our journey and offering prayers and encouragement. We hope our next update to be about matching with a beautiful mama and her baby. 😊

Blessings to you all!

Ben & Lydia Wright
