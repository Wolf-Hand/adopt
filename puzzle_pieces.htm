Puzzle Pieces!

We are selling puzzle pieces! Each piece will be $10, and you can purchase as many as you like! When you purchase one, we will write your name on the back of the puzzle piece. Once all of the puzzle pieces have been purchased, we will assemble the puzzle and place it in a two-way glass frame to be hung in our child's nursery. We want to be sure to let our baby know that we have a village of people who helped us bring them home.

Click the button above to let us know that you would like puzzle pieces!

You can make your payment ($10/per puzzle piece) by PayPal, Venmo, or cash if you live nearby!
