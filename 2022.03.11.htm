Wright Adoption

March 11, 2022

Moving Right Along

We got our Family Profile Book all completed today and sent off to be approved by our consultants!

Our home study worker contacted us today and said she plans to send us the final draft of our home study by Monday!

We are also in the middle of doing some extra background checks for our consultants so we can potentially match with birth mothers in Texas!

Lydia's grandma finished putting together the puzzle we got for our fundraiser! We are excited to get this framed and added to Baby's room!

We, again, want to thank everyone who has donated money, bought t-shirts, bath products, or puzzle pieces! We are absolutely overwhelmed with gratitude. It’s still hard to grasp how quickly we were able to raise this money with your help! I want to take some time to explain what it means when we say “we have reached our fundraising goal.”

When we were setting our budget, we landed on $40,000. This was the suggested budget by Faithful Adoption Consultants, our consulting agency. Typically, they see cases between $35,000-$55,000. Our agency will not present cases to us that exceed the budget we set for ourselves. This budget is for the actual costs of the adoption upon matching with a birth mother. Things that are not included in that amount: consultant contract, home study, background checks, finger printing, profile book, travel costs, etc. Any monies we raise above our initial budget/fundraising goal will go toward the other expenses list here!

Again, we are so thankful for all your support, prayers, and encouraging words!

Blessings to you all!

Ben & Lydia Wright
