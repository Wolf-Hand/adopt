Wright Adoption

March 3, 2022

Met Goals

We are unbelievably thrilled/blessed/amazed that we have met our fundraising goal!

We sincerely thank everyone who helped us reach this goal so quickly! Our minds have been absolutely blown by everyone's generosity! Your love and support mean more than we know how to put into words.

Also, we have officially completed our part of the home study process! Now we are just waiting for the paperwork to be completed on the agency's end. Ideally, we will get a draft of our home study sometime next week to review for any mistakes. After the final review by the home study agency, we will get the “official” home study indicating we are approved as adoptive parents!

We are hoping to be able to “go active” with our consulting agency by the end of March. This means we will be able to start reviewing potential matches and having our family book presented to birth mothers who are considering adoption as the parenting option for their children.

We thank you for all your prayers and encouragement as we continue the path to meeting our child!

Blessings to you all!

Ben & Lydia Wright
